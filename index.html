🎵 It's My Life – Useful Expressions

From lyrics → to real workplace English 🇧🇷➡️🇺🇸🇬🇧

Casual English Formal English Tradução (PT-BR) Business / Workplace Context
It's my life, take it or leave it This is my life; the choice is yours É a minha vida, aceite ou não Expressar autonomia sobre decisões pessoais ou profissionais.
Set me free, what's that crap Allow me to act independently; avoid nonsense instructions Me deixe livre, pare com essas bobagens Lidar com microgerenciamento de forma assertiva.
I got my own life, you got your own life I have my responsibilities and so do you Eu tenho minha própria vida, você tem a sua Delimitar responsabilidades entre colegas ou equipes.
Live your own life and set me free Please focus on your tasks and allow me to handle mine Viva sua própria vida e me deixe livre Pedir autonomia e evitar interferência em suas tarefas.
Mind your business and leave my business Kindly attend to your responsibilities and let me manage mine Cuide do seu negócio e deixe o meu Estabelecer limites claros de responsabilidades no ambiente profissional.
Very little knowledge is dangerous Partial knowledge can lead to mistakes Pouco conhecimento é perigoso Justificar necessidade de revisão, treinamento ou consulta a especialistas.
Stop bugging me / stop bothering me Please stop interrupting or disturbing me Pare de me incomodar / encher Solicitar que colegas parem de interromper; grau leve → médio.
Stop yelling me Please refrain from shouting at me Pare de gritar comigo Quando a voz alta afeta sua concentração; grau médio → forte.
Day and night Continuously / all the time Dia e noite Descrever esforços constantes ou sobrecarga de trabalho.
Stop telling me how to run your business Please do not instruct me on how to manage my responsibilities Pare de me dizer como administrar meu negócio / trabalho Delimitar autonomia; grau forte → muito forte.
Take a trip to east and west Explore different perspectives / environments Viaje para leste e oeste Incentivar aprendizado global ou benchmarking de mercado.
Tired of you Colleagues are fatigued by your behavior Cansado de você Feedback profissional para comportamento repetitivo ou improdutivo.

💼 Real Situations – Practice Using the Expressions

Imagine you are at work and a colleague keeps interrupting your tasks repeatedly.

Expressions you can use:

  • Stop bugging me / stop bothering me (grau leve → médio)
  • Stop yelling me (grau médio → forte)
  • Mind your business and leave my business (grau forte → muito forte)

Context: Assert boundaries depending on the intensity of the interference.

Your manager keeps trying to control minor decisions that are your responsibility.

Expressions you can use:

  • Stop telling me how to run your business (grau forte → muito forte)
  • I got my own life, you got your own life
  • It's my life, take it or leave it

Context: Showing autonomy and confidence in decision-making.

After long hours working on a project, you feel exhausted by repeated interruptions and unnecessary advice.

Expressions you can use:

  • Very little knowledge is dangerous
  • Tired of you
  • Live your own life and set me free

Context: Expressing frustration and boundaries in a professional but non-aggressive way.

You are collaborating with a global team and need to explore different strategies.

Expressions you can use:

  • Take a trip to east and west
  • Day and night

Context: Encouraging global exploration and continuous effort.
